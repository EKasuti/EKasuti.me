My Journey

Growing up, I always dreamed of becoming an architect - blending my passion for design and art with structural innovation.x But when I took COSC 001 with Professor Vasanta during my freshman fall at Dartmouth, everything changed. That first "Hello World" sparked a new passion, and I discovered that coding could be just as creative as drafting blueprints.

Since that pivotal moment, I've been on an exciting journey through computer science:

  • Research: Developing "Sprout" - a health-tracking mobile app at Empower Lab that supports individuals with chronic conditions like Parkinson's
  • Teaching: Worked as a CS001 Teaching Assistant where I guided students through their first coding experiences, helping them to understand the fundamentals of programming and problem-solving.
  • Development: Building impactful projects like:
    • QlikShift - A full-stack shift assignment system replacing manual Excel processes at Dartmouth Library
    • AI Helpdesk - An automated ticketing solution that won 2nd place at the Davis & Shirtliff Hackathon
    • Tiny Search Engine - A high-performance web crawler and indexer built in C
  • Internships: Collaborated with a team to build an end-to-end Young Tal website. Implemented APIs and integrated translated pages using ReactJS. The project led to 100+ sign ups and 18 young talents securing employment after deployment.

What began as curiosity in that introductory CS course has grown into a passion for solving problems through coding and design. I’m excited to continue this journey, exploring the intersection of technology and creativity.
